Hi! My name is Ben Sandler and this is my beloved go-torch! It is a stochastic flame profiler written in Go. Attach an image file and go-torch will stochastically (i.e. random) profile it! Curious if your image is a torch or not? If it is, is the torch cool or is it lame? Go-torch will help you find out once and for all!
